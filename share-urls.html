Don't Share These for Embeds

2 patterns

These URLs are working as designed but they aren't shareable. They serve the raw image / SPA shell directly with no embed wrapper.

Raw Image Poster PNG Endpoint
https://havenmap.online/api/posters/{type}/{key}.png
What it is
The raw PNG file. Used internally as the og:image for every embed above.
Why not share
It's a bare image — Discord shows just the picture with no title, description, or click target. Use the friendly URL instead (e.g. /community-stats/Haven, not /api/posters/og_community/Haven.png).
No SSR Direct /haven-ui/ URLs
https://havenmap.online/haven-ui/{anything}
What it is
The chromed SPA, served at the prefixed path that the app uses internally.
Why not share
Bot scrapers see the static SPA shell and emit the generic OG tags from index.html. To get a custom embed, share the bare-root URL (e.g. /community-stats/Haven, not /haven-ui/community-stats/Haven).
