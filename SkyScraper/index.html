New to the ARG?

Sixty-second catch-up. If you're seeing the words "Project Skyscraper" for the first time, start here.

An alternate reality game is happening in the No Man's Sky community. A website at project-skyscraper.com has been publishing what look like corrupted system logs since late March 2026. The author calls themselves The Architect.

The ETARC moderators surfaced the site publicly on May 15. By May 18, the site published a "security breach" log that includes a live hyperlink pointing directly back at the ETARC investigation thread. The fiction has named the investigators as part of the story.

"Project Skyscraper" is also the early codename for No Man's Sky itself, and the name of Hello Games' internal proprietary engine. Whoever The Architect is, they chose a name with very specific studio-internal weight.

The shape of it

Two phases so far:

  • Sleep phase (Mar 24 – Apr 30) — the site published a uniform "sleep mode" log every 2-3 days. Filler. Routine. Three small anomalies broke the pattern.
  • Breach phase (May 3 onward) — the logs shift to "security events." Pattern violations escalate. Connect1on (May 8), missing digit in 12/5/206 (May 12), the breach (May 18). Each anomaly looks deliberate.

The pieces in play

  • TR4CE.jpg — a static-noise image that hides text in the red channel. Recovered phrases: Access denied, Connection detected, Vision, Architect. Surfaced by red-channel isolation.
  • A Wallace Stevens pastiche poem backdated to 2005 — decoded by toddumptious p210 as instructions to look at "blue birds" (Twitter) and "blue sky" (Bluesky). The Architect maintains both.
  • A second WordPress site — theskyscraperarchitect-ywvhk.wordpress.com. Reserved but empty. Likely the next chapter's staging area.
  • An anomaly chain — five deliberate breaks in the site's format, each extracting a single digit. Two readings: 1-1-2 as the European emergency number, OR a descending countdown (4-3-2-1).
  • "Process ID: 16" — recurring in every full security log. Maps directly to Loop16 from the Waking Titan ARG (2017). The Loop16 AI is canonically an older version of the Atlas. This ARG is continuing established NMS canon.

Why this matters

The previous large NMS ARG, Waking Titan, set up much of what's now considered foundational lore — Atlas as a simulation, Loop16/Emily as a precursor AI, the Travellers as digitised brain scans. Project Skyscraper is engaging with the same canonical material at a level of detail that suggests either official involvement, or a creator with very deep familiarity with the franchise's deepest cuts.

The active investigation is on the ETARC forum thread. This page documents the work — it does not replace it.

TR4CE.jpg · the hidden-text image
solved
A static-noise image at /tr4ce/ with the caption "Memory_bloc_restoration: 0/365 Completed."
What surfaced
  • Isolating the red channel and pushing contrast reveals four phrases: denied, Connection detected, Vision (partial), Architect (partial). community consensus · ETARC pp. 10-12
  • The green channel renders Access denied more prominently than red — both channels carry text.
  • R-G channel difference reveals each phrase rendered twice, with the left rendering destroyed by overlay glitch and the right intact. Matches Sir_oops's earlier hypothesis. Sir_oops
  • The phrase Architect recovered from the image confirms the WordPress author's display name is the in-canon system identity, not a fan handle.

  • LSB / bit-plane steganography — only JPEG quantization artifacts present, no hidden bit-level encoding.

  • 2D FFT for hidden periodic content.

What "Project Skyscraper" actually meant to Hello Games

Two readings have surfaced and they aren't mutually exclusive.

Sean Murray and Grant Duncan, during Hello Games' Joe Danger years, framed their indie path as building "Swedish houses" before they could build a "Skyscraper." Sean used Project Skyscraper in early interviews to describe what would eventually become No Man's Sky. Clemm confirmed the framing on page 10. Clemm p199 · toddumptious p301

But ThatBomberBoi added on page 15: Skyscraper is also the actual name of Hello Games' proprietary engine, visible in a leaked debug build, and used in both No Man's Sky and Light No Fire. ThatBomberBoi p289

The codename is real. The engine is real. Choosing this name for an ARG is choosing a very specific, very studio-internal artifact.

Solo, or team?

The earliest reading was a single author. The multi-platform coordination — site, X, Bluesky, Twitch, second site, Reddit, ETARC presence — has made several investigators reconsider. William: "I don't think it's only one person behind it." Polyphemus: "It also reduces the chances we're dealing with a single individual." William p181 · Polyphemus p185

The evidence cuts both ways. The bulk-batched filler is consistent with one person managing routine; the live-written puzzles, the late-night monitoring, and the active forum lurking are all behaviours one person can sustain. But the breadth of platforms suggests delegation. Honest answer: we don't know.
