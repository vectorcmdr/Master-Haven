New to the ARG?

Sixty-second catch-up. If you're seeing the words "Project Skyscraper" for the first time, start here.

An alternate reality game is happening in the No Man's Sky community. A website at project-skyscraper.com has been publishing what look like corrupted system logs since late March 2026. The author calls themselves The Architect.

The ETARC moderators surfaced the site publicly on May 15. By May 18 it published a "security breach" log with a live hyperlink pointing straight back at the ETARC investigation thread — the fiction had named the investigators as part of the story. From there it turned interactive: the community solves the site's puzzles, and solving them visibly changes the site.

"Project Skyscraper" is also the early codename for No Man's Sky itself and the name of Hello Games' internal engine — a name with very specific studio weight.

Where it stands now (June 1): the community cracked a password gate with EMILY, the Architect declared "the door is open," the ARG stepped into the real world with a physical drop in London — and the AI Emily, from the 2017 Waking Titan ARG, returned with an audio recording. It increasingly looks like a Waking Titan revival.

The shape of it

Five phases so far:

  • Sleep phase (Mar 24 – Apr 30) — a uniform "sleep mode" log every 2-3 days. Filler, with three tiny anomalies breaking the pattern.
  • Breach phase (May 3 – 22) — the logs turn into "security events." Deliberate pattern-breaks escalate: Connect1on, the 12/5/206 missing digit, and the May 18 SECURITY_BREACH_CONFIRMED that links the ETARC thread.
  • Restoration phase (May 23 – 26) — pages gain a Current status. The foundational poem destabilizes, mutates six times, and is solved by the community (May 25), flipping to stable. A readout reveals the scope: 0/365 memory blocs to restore.
  • Reboot phase (May 27 – 30) — the system reboots (SECURITY_PROTOCOL_FAILURE), the Architect's private /inbox/ leaks (an email thread with a figure called Ghost), the homepage becomes a particle "neural core," a backdated page hands over a school cipher, and a password gate appears.
  • Contact phase (May 31 – June 1) — the gate falls to EMILY; a ciphered email reads "the door is open." Then reality starts glitching into the real world: a London location drop, the first physical operator run, and — on the /about/ page — Emily's actual voice.

The pieces in play

  • The Wallace Stevens poem (solved) — a pastiche backdated to 2005 that decoded to "blue birds → Twitter, blue sky → Bluesky," then mutated and resolved to the real "Sunday Morning." The first puzzle the community solved, and the first time community action changed the site.
  • The Architect & Ghost — an exposed /inbox/ shows the Architect corresponding with a figure named Ghost (address <null>), using a school cipher (MINDFAGEBJRLHCVPQSKYUWOXTZ). Latest message: LA PORTE EST OUVERTE — "the door is open."
  • The EMILY gate & the operator form — the password EMILY opened an External_Operator "memory-anchoring" form where the community submits memories to try to restore 0/365 blocs. No System response yet.
  • Reality glitching & the real world — a June-1 post tagged Location: LONDON sent an operator to the Atlas Building to recover a cassette. He found "only dust," and the Architect logged and reposted the run.
  • Emily's voice — the /about/ page now hides an audio recording of Emily (the Waking Titan AI) over a rising heartbeat, behind Morse code reading REAL.
  • "Process ID: 16" / Loop16 — recurring in every full security log; maps to Loop16 / Emily from Waking Titan (2017), canonically an older Atlas. This ARG is continuing established NMS canon.
  • TR4CE.jpg — the original static-noise image that hides text in the red channel (Access denied, Connection detected, Vision, Architect); its near-lossless quantization tables are still an open steganography puzzle.
  • A reserved second site — theskyscraperarchitect-ywvhk.wordpress.com, still empty. A likely staging area for what's next.

Why this matters

The previous large NMS ARG, Waking Titan (2017), set up much of what's now foundational lore — Atlas as a simulation, Loop16/Emily as a precursor AI, the Travellers as digitised minds. Project Skyscraper is engaging that same material at deep-cut level — and as of June 1 it has revived Emily's literal voice, staged a real-world drop, and seen Anashel (Waking Titan's puppetmaster) reappear as an active ETARC moderator. The "official vs fan-made" question is still open, but the signals increasingly point official.

The active investigation lives on the ETARC forum thread. This page documents the work — it does not replace it.

The JPEG quantization tables
active
A forensic pass over the site's image files found a hidden layer beneath the pictures themselves: all three core JPEGs are built with near-lossless quantization tables. vector_cmdr · community forensics
What's abnormal
  • The DQT (quantization) tables in TR4CE.jpg, IMG_00004.jpg (the QUOTE-page image), and project-skyscraper.jpg contain only the values 1, 2 and 3. Standard JPEG tables run 1–255 on a smooth gradient. Values this low mean the image is compressed near-losslessly — unheard of for a photo, and the textbook setup for surviving hidden LSB data through compression.
  • The tables aren't random: the non-1 cells form clean triangular / anti-diagonal bands — a deliberately constructed pattern, not a compression artifact.
The forensic link
  • TR4CE.jpg and IMG_00004.jpg share byte-identical quantization tables, ICC colour profile and progressive-scan pattern — same tool, same settings. This ties the QUOTE-page image directly to the TR4CE red-channel image.
  • project-skyscraper.jpg (the building) was made by the same tooling with different parameters — same 1–3 signature, different scan layout. One author, one pipeline, across all three images.

  • A full cipher battery against the table data — RC4, AES, Vigenère, Polybius, Trifid, RSA, Baconian, base-3/5, Feistel, Golomb — produced no coherent plaintext; standard LSB extraction across every channel found no readable text either.
