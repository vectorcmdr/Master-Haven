Help

Spectral Class

Letter (O/B/A/F/G/K/M/E/X/Y) + Number (0-9) + optional Suffix. Example: G2pf, M7, O3f. Visible in the Galactic Map's analysis visor.

Wealth Tier

★ = Low, ★★ = Medium, ★★★ = High, ☠ = Pirate. Required because it determines what trade goods the station can sell. Auto-set to None for Abandoned/None economies.

Conflict Level

🔥 to 🔥🔥🔥. Indicates how often you'll encounter Sentinel patrols and pirate attacks. Required for non-Abandoned economies.

Planet Attributes

Special toggles for unusual conditions: Has Rings, Dissonant, Infested, Extreme Weather, Water World, Vile Brood, Bubble Planet, Floating Islands, Gas Giant, plus resource flags (Ancient Bones, Salvageable Scrap, Storm Crystals, Gravitino Balls).

Exotic Trophy

Rare procedurally-generated objects sometimes found on planets — Bubble Cluster, Calcishroom, Hexplate Bush, etc. Only 11 exist game-wide.

★ Submit for Record Consideration

Auto-checks when your value beats the current Haven record. Uncheck if you don't want this submission flagged for wonder review. Records are computed from your data, not declared by you.
